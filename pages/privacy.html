Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

ℹ️ CampusPrime is committed to protecting the privacy of school administrators, teachers, students, and parents. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

1. Who We Are

CampusPrime ("we", "us", "our") is a school management software platform operated by 18Spar, based in India. Our platform is available at campusprime.in.

For privacy-related queries, contact us at: privacy@campusprime.in

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • School Administrator Data: Name, email address, phone number, school name, location.
  • Student Data: Student name, class, roll number, parent's phone number, annual fee amount. We do not collect student Aadhaar, date of birth, or academic records unless explicitly entered by the school.
  • Fee Records: Payment amounts, dates, fee types. We do not store card numbers or UPI PINs.
  • Attendance Data: Daily present/absent records per student per class.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, device type. Used to improve the product.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the CampusPrime platform
  • To send fee receipts and attendance alerts via WhatsApp (only with school admin's instruction)
  • To generate reports and analytics for school administrators
  • To process subscription payments via Razorpay
  • To send product updates and important notices to school administrators
  • To improve our product and fix bugs

We do not sell your data. We do not use student data for advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in Google Cloud's asia-south1 (Mumbai) region. We use:

  • Firestore security rules to enforce tenant isolation — each school can only access its own data
  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Scheduled daily backups to Google Cloud Storage

5. Your Rights Under DPDP Act 2023

As a data principal under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure (right to be forgotten) — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Nominate another individual to exercise these rights in case of incapacity or death
  • Grievance redressal — raise complaints about how we handle your data

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@campusprime.in with subject "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days.

6. Children's Data

CampusPrime collects student data (including data of minors) on behalf of schools. Schools are the "data fiduciaries" under DPDP Act for student data, and are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from parents/guardians before registering student information in CampusPrime.

We process student data solely as a "data processor" acting on behalf of the school, and only for the purposes of providing school management services.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Database, Authentication, Storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Razorpay — Payment processing (Privacy Policy)
  • WhatsApp — Message delivery (via school's own WhatsApp Business number)
  • Vercel — Website hosting (Privacy Policy)

8. Data Retention

We retain your school and student data for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days after cancellation. After this period, data is permanently deleted. You may request immediate deletion at any time.

9. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.
